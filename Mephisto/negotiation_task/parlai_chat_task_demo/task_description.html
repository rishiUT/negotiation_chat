"Negotiate the price of an item - earn up to $2!"


Disclaimer

This HIT is part of a UT Austin scientific research project. Your decision to complete this HIT is completely voluntary, and your responses are recorded as anonymous. The results of this research project may be shared publicly with other researchers. Since this HIT is a coordinated task between two crowd workers, we ask you to be polite and courteous in all your dealings with the other worker. Upon clicking the “Accept” button, you will be indicating that you are at least 18 years of age, voluntarily agreeing to participate in this HIT, and that you will be respectful of your coworker.


Requirements

In order to complete this task, you must be:

  • Using a laptop / PC equipped with a microphone
  • In a quiet environment
  • Using English as the language to hold the conversation


Motivation

The goal of this task is to negotiate the price of an item listed on Craigslist with the aim to maximize your own gain while also reaching an agreement with the other side.


For this purpose, you will be paired up with another crowd worker and will be assigned the role of a buyer or a seller. Your co-worker will be assigned the role complementary to yours (For example: If you are assigned the role of a buyer, your co-worker will be assigned the role of a seller and vice versa). The buyers will receive a bonus payment if they get the seller to agree for sale of an item at a given target price that is lower than the listing price. On the other hand, the sellers will receive a bonus payment if they get the buyer to pay for their item an amount as close as possible to the listing price.
